About myself

I am Jordi.

I studied mathematics at the Humboldt University. (To be more precise, I have a teaching bachelor's degree in physics and mathematics, and a master's degree in mathematics.)

I currently work as a software developer at tech@spree and as a substitute teacher for mathematics.

Because of my work at school and university – as a teacher or tutor, respectively – I have quite some teaching experience.

What I offer

I offer private math lessons for...

  • school – all years and all topics
  • university – all basic lectures (linear algebra, analysis, ...)

In particular (but not exclusively), I can help you with homework or exam preparation.

How much does a lesson cost?

A 60-minute lesson costs 30 euros.

You can pay in cash on site or in advance via PayPal.

When and where do the lessons take place?

The lessons usually take place...

  • in my apartment in Biesdorf at the whiteboard, or
  • online at the graphics tablet (for example, via Discord).

Lessons at the student's home are also possible in principle, given the distance is short, and subject to payment of the time needed for traveling.

As for the time, I am quite flexible.
