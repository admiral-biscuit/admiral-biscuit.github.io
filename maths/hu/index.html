Dirac Eigenvalues of Homogeneous 3-Spheres

In my thesis (inspired by results from Ch. Bär and E. Lauret, I found an explicit formula for the smallest absolute Dirac eigenvalue for each left-invariant metric on the homogeneous 3-sphere SU(2) of positive scalar curvature (!), and I proved that two such spheres are Dirac isospectral if and only if they are isometric.

Read the thesis: Dirac Eigenvalues of Homogeneous 3-Spheres

My dear professor Dorothee Schüth further improved and extended the work, and converted it into a paper.

Read the paper: On the Dirac Spectrum of Homogeneous 3-Spheres
